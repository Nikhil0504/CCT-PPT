What is CCT?

What is CCT?

We are an organization of teens who aspire to make india safe and covid free again.

They call us the “ Future of India” but does that mean we do nothing in the present?

How we work?

How we work?

A collaborative effort by young, responsible teen minds in an encouraging, inclusive environment ensures that everyone's unique ideas and thoughts are heard and moved towards implementation for a healthier, more efficient India.
